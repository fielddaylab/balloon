Hot Air Balloon Sim

Description

  • The top-most green button fires the engine and heats the air in the balloon
  • The middle green button releases a bit of hot air from the top, causing it to cool
  • The bottom green button cuts the rope that holds the balloon to the ground
  • Inside the red balloon, the blue vertical line indicates vertical force/acceleration due to lift, and the black horizontal line indicates the horizontal force/acceleration due to wind.

To Play

  1. When the balloon rises, it will get stuck at a certain altitude due to an invisible rope
  2. Cut the rope with the bottom button
  3. See how long you can control the altitude of the balloon
  4. Refresh your browser to restart
